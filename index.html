A Visual Guide to NYC Restaurant and Health Inspection Data

About Our Website

Welcome to NYC Food & Health, your ultimate tool for exploring restaurant options through an engaging, data-driven experience. Our platform combines map-based exploration with interactive data visualizations to help you identify and compare restaurants in your current location or specific destinations for future planning. View restaurants displayed on a map, where a simple hover reveals essential details like name, rating, and cuisine type. Use toggle options to compare neighborhoods or boroughs by average rating or inspection score.

With our interactive bar chart, you can explore the distribution of cuisines within selected areas and dive deeper by selecting bars to see restaurants within specific cuisine categories in your chosen borough or neighborhood. Clicking into a specific restaurant reveals comprehensive details, including review counts and inspection scores over time. Dive into historical inspection data to view total violations, violations per inspection, and score changes across inspections, providing insights into the consistency and quality of restaurant standards. Final Vis Collection empowers you to make informed dining decisions with ease and confidence.

Our Data

Our project brings together a robust dataset by joining information from multiple sources, using address as the primary key to link and integrate details seamlessly. We are sourcing NYC restaurant data from Kaggle, specifically from a dataset created by user beridzeg45, which provides key information on restaurant names, ratings, and cuisine types. Additionally, we are incorporating inspection results from the Department of Health and Mental Hygiene (DOHMH) New York City Restaurant Inspection Results, accessible through NYC Open Data. This data includes valuable insights into inspection scores, violation details, and trends over time. By merging these datasets, we aim to provide a comprehensive, reliable resource for exploring and comparing dining options across New York City.

Objective 2: Analyze Cuisine Distributions by Neighborhood and Borough

Experience New York City's vibrant dining scene through our interactive map that visualizes restaurant distributions by borough and neighborhood. This feature allows you to delve into specific areas of the city, comparing the density and variety of restaurants across different locales. Whether you're a local looking to explore new neighborhoods or a visitor planning your culinary journey, our interactive map provides an intuitive and engaging way to navigate the city's diverse food landscape.

Objective 3: Understand inspection violations at the restaurant level.

In the following faceted visualization, the chart on the left contains the New York City neighborhood choropleth overlaid with restaurant locations. A color encoding is included that defines whether the restaurant has most recently been awared an inspection grade of A, B, or C. The chart on the right is a horizontal bar graph that includes restaurant inspections, broken out by individual violation codes. A color encoding is included to indicate the average number of points associated with that violation code, as violation codes can be awarded a range of points.
